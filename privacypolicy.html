Privacy Policy for WiFi Server Pro

Last Updated: January 15, 2025
Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Important: WiFi Server Pro operates entirely on your local network and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

WiFi Server Pro does NOT collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • User accounts or profiles
  • Browsing history or usage analytics

1.2 File Access

The app accesses files on your device solely for the purpose of sharing them on your local network. Files remain on your device and are only accessible through the local server you create.

1.3 Network Information

The app accesses your local network information (IP address, WiFi network name) only to establish local server connections. This information is not stored or transmitted outside your device.

2. How We Use Information

WiFi Server Pro uses accessed information exclusively for:

  • Creating HTTP and FTP servers on your local network
  • Displaying files and folders for local sharing
  • Generating QR codes for easy connection
  • Showing server status and connection information

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Operation

All app operations occur locally on your device and network:

  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • Files remain on your device at all times
  • Server connections are limited to your local WiFi network
  • No remote access or internet connectivity is required

3.2 Security Measures

  • Optional password protection for server access
  • HTTPS encryption support for secure connections
  • Network isolation - servers only accessible on local WiFi
  • No external data transmission or cloud storage

4. Permissions Required

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read and share files from your device
  • Network Access: To create local HTTP/FTP servers
  • WiFi State: To detect network connectivity
  • Foreground Service: To keep servers running in background
  • Notifications: To show server status updates

All permissions are used solely for local file sharing functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

WiFi Server Pro does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media platforms
  • Crash reporting services

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or disclose any information because:

  • No personal data is collected
  • No usage analytics are gathered
  • No external servers are contacted
  • All operations are local to your device and network

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WiFi Server Pro is safe for users of all ages:

  • No personal information is collected from anyone, including children under 13
  • No user accounts or profiles are created
  • No online interactions or communications are enabled
  • The app operates entirely offline on local networks

8. International Data Transfers

Since WiFi Server Pro does not collect or transmit any data to external servers, there are no international data transfers.

9. Data Retention

WiFi Server Pro does not retain any user data because:

  • No data is collected or stored by the app
  • Files remain on your device under your control
  • App settings are stored locally on your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • File Access: Choose which folders to share
  • Server Control: Start/stop servers at any time
  • Network Access: Control who can access your server
  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all traces

11. Security Best Practices

To ensure your privacy and security:

  • Use strong passwords when enabling authentication
  • Only share servers on trusted WiFi networks
  • Stop servers when not in use
  • Be cautious about which folders you choose to share
  • Regularly review connected devices on your network

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in legal requirements
  • App feature updates
  • Improved privacy practices

Updated policies will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
